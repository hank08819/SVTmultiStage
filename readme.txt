
Three datasets used in "Singular Value Thresholding Multi-stage Matrix Completion for Drug Sensitivity" (Yang, X, Tag, X, Li, C, Han, H)
 
1) rM-294C-23D_noNA.csv is the dataset of CCLE_AA（294cells*23drugs）;
2) CCLE_IC50.csv  is the dataset of CCLE_IC50 (363cells*24drugs);
3) GDSC_IC50.csv  is  the dataset of GDSC_IC50 (555cells*98drugs);
